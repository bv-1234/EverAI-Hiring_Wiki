What We Do

How It Started 🚀

It all started when our co-founders, Michael and Thomas, set out to create a WhatsApp chatbot assistant. But as they dove deeper into analyzing how users were interacting with it, they stumbled upon something surprising: people weren't just using it for assistance, they were using it romantically! 💘

That "aha" moment sparked a bold new direction. Instead of sticking with the original plan, Michael and Thomas took a leap and decided to build something unique: Candy.ai, a platform that helps people foster deeper, more meaningful connections.

Our Product & Technology

We believe that AI isn't just about answering questions, it's about creating experiences that feel alive. We're focused on developing technology that allows users to step into stories, create characters, and explore their fantasies in a safe, fun, and judgment-free environment.

Candy.ai & YumeAI

Our journey began with Candy🍬, the world's leading realistic AI companion platform, and has now expanded with YumeAI, our dedicated platform for stylized and anime aesthetics.

Across both platforms, we provide users with an array of tools to design and engage with AI companions in ways that reflect their personal vision.

Here is what our technology allows users to do:

  • Design & Customize: Users can design their own AI companions from the ground up, customizing appearance, personality traits, and voice, making each character truly unique.
  • Chat through Text & AI Phone Calls: We take immersion to the next level. With just one tap, users can connect through a phone call and experience the full power of the AI they created, like talking to an old friend or meeting an exciting new one.
  • Generate High-Fidelity Visuals: Users can describe a scene or idea, and our engine brings it to life. Whether they want a picture or a short video, a simple prompt generates a unique visual tailored to their imagination.

Pushing the Boundaries of Immersion 🚀

We don't just settle for "standard" features. We are constantly shipping industry-first innovations to make the experience deeper and more engaging:

  • Advanced Image & Video Tech: We've enabled users to turn static images (including Anime styles) into motion with 5-second and 10-second animations.
  • Group Chat: Users can now step into multi-character dynamics, where companions interact not just with the user, but with each other! 🤯
  • Hyper-Realistic "Live Action": We have introduced Live Action models that blur the line between AI and reality, offering a level of visual detail that feels completely authentic.
  • The Explore Feed: We created an engaging Feed where users can discover high-quality content and characters created by our world-class content teams, providing endless inspiration and entertainment.
  • Memory System: It's not just a chat bot; it remembers. Users can now build long-term context with their companions, who recall specific details and shared moments to build a genuine bond.
  • Creative Mode (New! 🎨): We recently launched Creative Mode, giving users a "sandbox" to generate images and videos freely from prompts without being tied to a specific character. It's pure generative freedom.

How It's Going 🔥

Since our launch in September 2023, Candy AI has absolutely taken off, becoming the largest platform of its kind worldwide in record time!

With 20 million unique users per month, we've earned our place among the top 4 Generative AI web products globally, standing shoulder-to-shoulder with the biggest names in the industry.

In under a year, we've hit eight-figure annualized revenue, a milestone that speaks to the incredible growth of our platform. Now, over 18 months in, we have scaled significantly past that milestone.

And we're not the only ones who've noticed: investors have too! We've successfully raised $10 million in funding, cementing Candy.ai as one of the fastest-growing companies in the space.

Our community of 50 Million users is a testament to the platform's incredible appeal and impact, particularly in two of the fastest-growing Gen AI categories: content generation and companion.

According to Harvard Business Review, Generative AI in 2025 has shifted from technical use cases to emotional, personal, and human-centric applications.

🧠 Bottom Line

CandyAI isn't just riding the wave, it's shaping it.

In a world where users are actively turning to GenAI for companionship, intimacy, and purpose, CandyAI is building exactly what people are asking for, and doing it earlier and better than most.

We couldn't be prouder of how far we've come, and can't wait to see what the future holds for us! And the best part? We're just getting started!
