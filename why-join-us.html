Reality Check: Is EverAI Right For You?

We value transparency above all else. EverAI is an incredible place to grow, but it is not for everyone. We are a high-performance team moving at breakneck speed.

We want all our team members to be happy and motivated at work, so to save your time and ours, please read the following.

You might not enjoy working here if:

🚧

You prefer heavy structure and fixed processes
We build the plane while flying it. Processes change, priorities shift, and we often have to figure things out on the fly. If you need a strict playbook or a manager to tell you exactly what to do every day, this probably won't be a good place for you.

🐌

You have a low tolerance for fast iteration
We ship features fast (sometimes in hours, not weeks). We test constantly. If you need 100% certainty or prefer to spend weeks perfecting a plan before executing, our pace will feel uncomfortable.

🙅

You expect a rigid role without ownership
We value "Own It, All of It". We operate with clear ownership. If something is broken, we fix it. We trust A-players to step outside their lane to fix problems and make decisions without waiting for approval. If you prefer strict job descriptions or rely on getting everyone to agree before moving forward, this isn't the environment for you.

🫣

You dislike being accountable and humble
With great freedom comes great responsibility. We trust you to work from anywhere, but we expect you to own your outcomes. If things go wrong, we expect you to raise your hand, learn, and fix it, not hide it.

🧪

You are uncomfortable with experimentation, ambiguity, or frequent change
We take risks. We try different things constantly to see what happens. Some things work; some don't. You need to be resilient enough to fail, learn, and try again immediately.
